# Pharmacy-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze over-the-counter (OTC) pharmacy sales data. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, product demand, sales representative productivity, geographic distribution, and monthly sales trends, helping stakeholders make data-driven business decisions.
# Tools & Technologies
Power BI

Power Query

DAX

Data Modeling

Data Visualization

# Objectives
Monitor overall sales performance.

Identify top-selling products and highest revenue contributors.

Evaluate sales representatives based on shipment volume.

Analyze product distribution across countries.

Track monthly order trends and sales patterns.

# Key Insights
Generated a total revenue of $58.9K from OTC product sales.

Distributed 3,488 boxes across multiple markets.

Identified Digestive Enzyme as the highest revenue-generating product.

Compared sales performance of 7 sales representatives and highlighted top contributors.

Analyzed product distribution across countries including the USA, UK, Canada, India, and Australia.

Revealed monthly fluctuations in order volume, with peak activity occurring during May and July.
